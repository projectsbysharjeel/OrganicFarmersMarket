Sink your teeth into some farm-fresh produce at these nearby farms in NSW, Australia. Have a look below and pick your favorite farms

You don't have to slave away for months in your garden to get super fresh fruits and vegetables. Head out of town and pick your favorite farms in New South Wales on OFM now.

Most Famous Farms

The following list is given for some of the famous Organic farms in New South Wales

  • Smith's Farming

    Smith's Farming is located in Maitland and has been growing pineapples, in the heart of pineapple country. It was founded by Jeff Said in 1960's

    PH. NUMBER: 0474883547

  • Loco Cherry Farms

    Loco cherry farms has a dedicated team of 40 staff not only nurture the farm but also provide guest services including guided tours, cafe dining, and specialist retail. Loco farms is owned by Jerry Loco since 1988.

    PH. NUMBER: 0420343197

  • Daisy Mango farms

    Daisy mango farms was founded in 2003 by Daisy shah, with a wealth of experience in fresh fruit production specially in mangoes. Today, It is one of the leading distributor around Australia.

    PH. NUMBER: 0451839200

  • Festive Veggie Farms

    Festive Veggie Farms is being operated by Kelly; daughter of late Bob lee since 25 years. The main mission is to produce rich quality veggies with the help of Solar panels; which is just installed to reduce the carbon foot print on earth.

    PH. NUMBER: 0451645687

  • Malinda's Farming

    Malinda's Farm is is being owned my Malinda since 12 years. The farm is famous for its super fine quality of vegetables. This farm has 3 branches all over Australia.

    PH. NUMBER: 0405788547
